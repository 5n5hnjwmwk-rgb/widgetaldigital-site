Privacy Policy for YAWA CAN

Effective date: March 13, 2026

YAWA CAN respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

Information We Collect

YAWA CAN does not require you to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

The app may use the following information solely to provide weather features:

  • Your selected or searched locations
  • Your current location, if you grant location permission
  • Your app preferences, such as forecast length or display settings

How Information Is Used

Location and preference data are used only to provide weather forecasts, radar, and related app functionality.

Location Data

If you allow location access, YAWA CAN uses your device location to provide local weather information. Location access is optional and can be changed at any time in your device settings.

Data Storage

Saved locations and app preferences may be stored locally on your device. This information is used only for app functionality and is not sold or shared for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

YAWA CAN retrieves weather and radar data from third-party providers, including Open-Meteo and RainViewer. Requests to those services may include location-related query data needed to return weather results.

Use of those services is also subject to their own privacy policies and terms.

Analytics and Tracking

YAWA CAN does not use third-party advertising SDKs and does not track you across apps or websites.

Children’s Privacy

YAWA CAN is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
