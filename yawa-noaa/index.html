Yet Another Weather App – NOAA
Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026

YAWA NOAA does not collect, sell, or share any personal data. The developer has no access to any information about you or your device.

Location

The app may request access to your device's location in order to retrieve nearby weather conditions, forecasts, and alerts from public weather services. Your location is used solely to make these data requests and is never stored, logged, or transmitted to the developer or any third party.

Data Processing

All calculations and data processing occur entirely on your device. YAWA NOAA uses no accounts, no analytics, no tracking technologies, and no advertising SDKs of any kind.

Third-Party Services

Weather data is fetched directly from public providers including NOAA/National Weather Service (weather.gov), Open-Meteo (open-meteo.com), and RainViewer (rainviewer.com). YAWA NOAA does not control and is not responsible for the privacy practices of these services. No data about you is sent to these providers beyond the coordinates needed to retrieve weather information.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
