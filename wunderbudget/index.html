Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Your financial data never leaves your device. wunderBudget has no servers, no accounts, and no tracking.

Overview

wunderBudget is a personal budgeting app built with your privacy in mind. All data you enter is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server or third party.

Data Collection

wunderBudget does not collect, transmit, or store any personal or financial data on external servers. All data — including transactions, categories, budgets, and imported bank data — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Bank Data and CSV Imports

When you import a CSV file from your bank, that data is processed entirely on your device. No transaction data, account numbers, or financial information is ever sent to wunderBudget's servers or any third party.

Analytics and Tracking

wunderBudget does not use any analytics frameworks, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. We do not track your usage of the app in any way.

Third-Party Services

wunderBudget does not integrate with any third-party services that would have access to your data.

iCloud

wunderBudget does not currently use iCloud sync. All data remains local to the device on which it was entered.

Children's Privacy

wunderBudget is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
